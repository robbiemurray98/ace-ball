Game Rules

First team to 24 wins. You must win by 2!

Set Up

Set up your boards 24ft apart.(more or less depending on skill set)

Choose teams

Form 2 teams. Each team member stands on opposite sides.

Play

Players must throw behind the front of board.

To score player must attempt to throw football with the result of hitting the board and opposition failing to catch ball off bounce (1 point) player throwing and successfully making into top hole (3 points) or bottom hole worth (6 points) If throwing team fails to hit board the result is 0.

The player catching must only use 1 hand to secure catch with no help of body.

If the ball enters the hole but bounces out due to ground impact, points are still awarded.

Player who are not involved in active play should step aside to give other players room.

The losing team gets 1 redemption throw to bring it within 2 points to keep the game alive.

If the losing team closes the gap to within 2 points, their score returns to the previous score.

*The above rules are subject to change to fit the players needs(closer boards, 2 hands for catching, defense on catches, etc.)
